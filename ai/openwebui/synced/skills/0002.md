# ERIDU Operational Metrics Framework
**Management Tracking & Team Guidance**

This document consolidates all operational metrics across ERIDU's three pillars — Erify (EF), ERISA (EA), and Commerce — plus Finance and HR. It serves two purposes: give management a clear tracking framework, and give each team explicit guidance on what they are accountable for.

**Version:** April 2026 | **Owner:** Country Manager | **Review:** Monthly

---

## 1. North Star Metrics

These are the metrics that matter most for ERIDU as a whole. All pillar metrics should ladder up to these.

| Metric | What We Track | Benchmark / Target |
|---|---|---|
| Total GMV | Gross Merchandise Value across all pillars and platforms (TikTok Shop + Shopee) | Primary north star — trending up month-on-month |
| Revenue by Pillar | EF revenue, EA revenue, Commerce revenue — monthly actuals vs forecast | Each pillar trending toward independent profitability |
| Overall Gross Margin | Total revenue minus all variable costs (MC, creator, campaign costs) | Improving trend; pillar-level breakdown in monthly P&L |
| Overall Net Margin | Gross margin minus fixed costs (payroll, rent, tools) | Positive trajectory as GMV scales |
| Revenue Concentration Risk | Top 5 clients/creators as % of total company revenue | Reduce concentration over time; flag if >40% from single source |
| Cash Conversion Cycle | DSO − DPO + DCO: how fast cash returns after costs paid | Shorten DSO, extend DPO — critical while cash is TIGHT |

## 2. Erify (EF) — Operational Metrics

*Source: EF P&L review (finance-confidential). Reviewed monthly by Head of Content + Country Manager.*

| Operational Metric | What We Track |
|---|---|
| Production SLA Fulfillment Rate | Five dimensions tracked with hard targets: missing shows 0%, late shows 0%, violations (platform or brand policy) 0%, LoS approval rate 100%, ticket fulfillment rate 100% |
| Scheduling Efficiency | Dual-MC slot rate: % of show slots using two MCs simultaneously — target: < 1%. Campaign MC usage rate: % of show slots filled by Campaign MCs (last resort tier) — target: < 5% |
| Total Livestreams | Total number of livestreams produced in the month, with breakdown: standard vs premium |
| GMV by Show Type & Account | GMV performance broken down by show type (standard / premium) and by client account |

## 3. ERISA (EA) — Operational Metrics

*Source: EA P&L review (finance-confidential). Reviewed monthly by Head of Creator + Country Manager.*

| Operational Metric | What We Track |
|---|---|
| GMV from Managed Creators | Total GMV generated by managed creators, broken down by tier (Tier 1–6) |

| GMV from Affiliate Campaigns | Total GMV from ADP affiliate campaigns, broken down by individual campaign |
| ADP Active Campaign Count | Number of affiliate (ADP) campaigns currently active |

## 5. Finance Operations

*Finance tracks cash health, billing accuracy, and compliance timeliness. All metrics below are owned by Finance, reviewed by Country Manager.*

| Metric | What We Track | Benchmark / Target |
|---|---|---|
| Collection Rate | Amount received vs amount invoiced — monthly | 100% collection; chase overdue aggressively |
| AR Aging | Outstanding receivables by age bucket: 7 / 14 / 30 / 60 days | Zero items beyond 30 days; 7-day threshold = escalation |
| AP Accuracy | Payments released on schedule per finance calendar | 100% on-time per calendar |
| Reconciliation Completion | All payment batches (MC, creator, Shopee SLP) reconciled 5 days before each payment release date | 5 days before each payment date — no exceptions |
| Monthly Close Timeliness | Monthly close completed within first 2 weeks | By 14th of following month |
| Petty Cash Accuracy | Receipts vs float balance | Zero unexplained variance; 10K float, 2K per transaction max |
| Filing On-Time Rate | WHT (15th), VAT (23rd), SSO (15th) — all filings on time | 100% — zero late filings, zero penalties |
| Cash Runway | Current cash reserve vs monthly burn; weeks/months of runway | Reported weekly while TIGHT; monthly while NORMAL |
| DSO | Days Sales Outstanding — average days for clients to pay | Minimize; enforce NET 0 defaults |
| DPO | Days Payable Outstanding — average days to pay vendors | Maximize within contract terms |
| Cash Conversion Cycle | DSO − DPO + DCO | Shorten continuously; reviewed weekly while TIGHT |
| Payroll Accuracy | Salary, incentives, deductions match approved amounts | Zero errors; any discrepancy reported immediately |

## 6. HR Operations

*HR metrics focus on compliance accuracy and recruiting velocity. Owned by HR, reviewed by Country Manager.*

| Metric | What We Track | Benchmark / Target |
|---|---|---|
| Payroll On-Time Rate | Payroll processed and paid by 10th of each month | 100% on-time |
| Filing Accuracy | SSO, WHT, and all HR-related filings filed correctly and on time | Zero errors; zero late filings |
| Recruiting Target Attainment | Roles filled vs open headcount target | vs monthly hiring plan |
| Time to Fill | Days from role opened to offer accepted | Tracked per role; benchmark to be set |
| Probation Completion Rate | New hires completing probation (119-day contract / 90-day internal) | Track early exits; review onboarding quality if high |
| PIP Resolution Rate | PIPs resolved (improvement or exit) vs ongoing | No PIP should remain open beyond defined timeline |
| Attendance & Tardiness Rate | Unexcused absences and tardiness incidents per team | Tracked by team lead; escalation per policy |
| Contractor Document Compliance | % of active contractors with complete documents on file before onboarding | 100% — no contractor works without signed docs |

## 7. Management Review Cadence

*Finance prepares all reports but does not attend pillar P&L reviews.*

| Review | Participants | Key Metrics Focus | Cadence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Daily Standup | Head of Pillar + Team (each pillar independently) | Yesterday's results, today's priorities, blockers | Daily |
| Weekly Team Review | Head of Pillar + Team Leads | Pipeline progress, delivery status, capacity, escalations | Weekly |
| Weekly Business Review | Each Head of Pillar + Country Manager | Performance vs plan, issues requiring CM decision | Weekly |
| EF P&L Review | Head of Content + Country Manager | Production SLA fulfillment rate, scheduling efficiency (dual-MC rate, campaign MC rate), total livestreams (standard/premium), GMV by show type/account | Monthly |
| EA P&L Review | Head of Creator + Country Manager | Creator GMV/recruitment/churn, superstar & non-superstar activeness, paid-package on-time delivery, ADP GMV, ADP active campaigns | Monthly |
| Commerce Metrics Review | Head of Commerce + Country Manager | GMV fulfillment rate, ticket fulfillment, sales target achievement rate, sales outreach count, new vendor onboarding rate | Monthly |
| Finance Metrics Review | Country Manager | Collection rate, AR aging, AP accuracy, reconciliation, monthly close, petty cash, filing on-time, cash runway, DSO, DPO, CCC, payroll accuracy | Monthly |
| HR Metrics Review | Country Manager | Payroll on-time, filing accuracy, recruiting target, time to fill, probation completion, PIP resolution, attendance, contractor doc compliance | Monthly |
| Overall P&L Review | Country Manager + Founder | Total GMV, revenue by pillar, fixed cost trend, net margin, cash position | Monthly |
| QBR — Pillar | Head of Pillar + Country Manager | Quarterly results vs targets, strategic progress, budget reforecast | Quarterly |
| QBR — Company | Country Manager + Founder | Company-wide QBR, all pillars, next quarter priorities | Quarterly |
| Annual Planning | Founder + Country Manager + Heads | Annual targets, market positioning, pillar growth plans, investment priorities | Annual |

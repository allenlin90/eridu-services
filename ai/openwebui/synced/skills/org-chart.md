## Contractor pools

Contractors do not have individual DMDs. Their processes are documented by the managing role.

| Pool | Managed by | Pillar | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| MCs — Standard | Talent Manager | EF | Primary workforce, target <250 THB/hour |
| MCs — Flexible | Talent Manager | EF | Peak/campaign overflow |
| MCs — Campaign | Talent Manager | EF | Last resort, up to 500 THB/hour, <5% usage |
| Operators (EFPT) (×7–10) | Production Manager | EF | Shift-based, rotating duty manager responsibility |
| Managed creators | Creator Services | EA | ~4,000+ MCN, under management contract |
| Campaign-only creators | Campaign team | EA | No management contract, affiliate campaigns |
| Outsourced creators | Campaign team | EA | External via agencies, overflow |
